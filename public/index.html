First Aid Standards June 2024

International First Aid Guidelines 2020

The International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) and the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) have released comprehensive first aid guidelines that serve as the foundation for high-quality, evidence-based first aid education worldwide, building safer and healthier communities through effective emergency response.

These comprehensive evidence-based first aid guidelines serve as the authoritative foundation for first aid program designers worldwide, providing scientifically validated practices and resuscitation protocols. Developed through collaboration between IFRC and ICRC, these harmonized standards ensure consistency across 192 Red Cross Red Crescent National Societies while allowing adaptation to local contexts, enabling communities to build safer, healthier environments through effective emergency response training and education programs.

First Aid Initiative September 2023

World First Aid Day - First Aid in the Digital World

World First Aid Day provides an annual opportunity for everybody to promote and gain knowledge in first aid. The 2023 theme "First Aid in the Digital World" highlights how technology can enhance first aid training and emergency response globally.

World First Aid Day demonstrates extraordinary global reach, impacting over 4.4 million people through coordinated initiatives involving more than 100 National Societies and 1,120 branches worldwide. With 90% of National Societies implementing the annual theme and 20 million people trained globally, this movement showcases the power of unified first aid education in building resilient communities and empowering individuals with life-saving emergency response skills across diverse cultural contexts.
